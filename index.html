Getting Started

Connect YouWire to Claude in 3 steps.

1

Get your MCP Server URL

Your URL is provisioned when your YouWire account is created. Format: https://[your-org].youwire.jp/mcp
Contact support@youwire.jp if you need help finding it.

2

Add as a Custom Connector in Claude

Go to claude.ai/customize/connectors → click + Add custom connector → enter your YouWire MCP URL.

3

Authenticate with OAuth

Complete the OAuth 2.1 flow using your YouWire credentials. Once connected, Claude has access to your call recordings.
